## Materials and Techniques That Matter

The quality of a frame depends heavily on the materials and craftsmanship involved. Premium framing services use durable materials such as hardwood, aluminum, and high-grade acrylic. Protective glass, often UV-resistant, helps prevent fading caused by sunlight, while acid-free mounts protect against discoloration. Skilled framers use precise cutting tools and assembly techniques to achieve a flawless finish. These elements are essential for creating frames that are both visually appealing and long-lasting.
